⬡ Core Features

Technology That Transforms Medical Training

From lifelike tissue simulation to moment-by-moment performance analytics — everything a surgeon needs before the day of the operation.

🫀

Ultra-Precise Anatomical Simulation

3D organ models built from thousands of real patient datasets, with realistic tissue texture, haptic feedback, and fluid dynamics for blood and body fluids.

⚡

Real-Time Complication Detection

An AI engine monitors every move and flags dangerous situations instantly — explaining how the complication arose and guiding the surgeon through the correct response.

📊

Detailed Performance Reports

After each session, receive a comprehensive report analyzing every movement, comparing your technique against expert benchmarks, and highlighting areas for improvement.

🎓

Specialized Training Curricula

Programs co-designed with leading surgical centers worldwide, covering specialties from cardiovascular and thoracic surgery to neurosurgery and orthopedics.

🌐

Multi-Surgeon Collaboration

An entire surgical team can train simultaneously in the same virtual OR, simulating real-world teamwork, communication, and role coordination under pressure.

🔁

Critical Moment Replay

Record any moment during training and replay it frame-by-frame for deep analysis, or share clips with mentors and colleagues for peer review and teaching.

⬡ Complication Management

Prepare for Every Possible Scenario

SurgVR exposes you to the most critical complications in a completely safe environment, so you know exactly how to respond when it matters most.

High Risk

🩸 Acute Hemorrhage

Simulation of major vascular injury with training in rapid hemorrhage control techniques and hemodynamic pressure management.

High Risk

💔 Sudden Cardiac Events

Intraoperative arrhythmia and cardiac arrest scenarios with immediate intervention protocols and defibrillation simulation.

Medium Risk

🫁 Anesthesia Complications

Simulation of allergic reactions, hypoxia, and airway obstruction during anesthesia, with step-by-step intervention guidance.

Medium Risk

🧠 Unintended Nerve Damage

Identification of critical nerve pathways, avoidance techniques, and management protocols for accidental neural injury.

Manageable

🦠 Infection Risk

Sterile field protocols, early sepsis recognition, wound contamination scenarios, and evidence-based prevention strategies.

Manageable

⚡ Equipment Failure

Managing power outages, device malfunctions, and instrument failure mid-procedure with emergency contingency protocols.
